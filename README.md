# myrecipes

A personal recipe app — keep all your recipes in one place, log what you cook, and build your shopping list automatically.

**Open the app: [myrecipes-pi.vercel.app](https://myrecipes-pi.vercel.app)**

---

## How to get started

### 1. Open the app

Go to [myrecipes-pi.vercel.app](https://myrecipes-pi.vercel.app) on your phone or computer. No download needed.

### 2. Sign in

Tap **Sign in**, enter your email address, and tap **Send magic link**. Open the email you receive and tap the link inside — you're signed in. No password to remember.

### 3. Add it to your home screen (optional)

This makes the app open full-screen, just like a normal app from the store.

- **iPhone (Safari):** tap the Share button → scroll down → **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**
- **Android (Chrome):** tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) → **Add to Home screen** → **Add**

---

## What you can do

| Feature | Where to find it |
| --- | --- |
| Browse and search your recipes | **Recipes** tab |
| Add a new recipe | **+** button (bottom-right) |
| Edit a recipe | Open it → tap **Edit** (top-right) |
| Shopping list | **List** tab |
| Log a cook and rate a recipe | Inside any recipe → **Log** tab |
| See your cooking stats | **Stats** tab |
| Plan meals across multiple recipes | **Plan** tab |
| Change language, theme, units, back up your data | **Settings** tab |
